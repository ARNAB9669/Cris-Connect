About Cris-Connect


Offline Connectivity

Our app runs entirely without internet or cellular networks, creating a local, resilient communication hub during natural disasters, power outages, and in remote areas.

Intelligent Assistance

AI monitors weather for early disaster alerts, enables SOS calls with live location, and can even detect critical scenarios during calls to dispatch emergency services.

Community & Healthcare

A community-driven map allows users to share real-time updates on shelters, aid, and hazards. A dedicated doctor section enables vital video consultations during emergencies.


Efficient & Accessible

Designed for low maintenance and cost-efficiency, Cris-Connect aims to provide a sustainable and accessible solution for communities, ensuring connectivity for all.

Local Area Network (LAN)

Each covered area operates as its own independent LAN, providing a dedicated "hotspot" for the entire village or community, ensuring stable and reliable local communication.
